Company Overview
01
Who We Are
A Four-Decade Legacy
of Precision Engineering

Established in 1985 by an engineer-entrepreneur with a clear vision — Solanke Enterprises has grown from a single-machine workshop into one of North Karnataka's most capable large-diameter precision machining facilities. Today, we are a ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing partner serving valve OEMs, pump manufacturers, and flow equipment companies across India.

Our 10,000 sq. ft. purpose-built facility in the Gamanagatti Industrial Estate, Hubballi, operates with a team of 15+ skilled machinists, quality engineers, and tooling experts. We process raw castings, forgings, and billets into dimensionally verified, ready-to-assemble components — with full material traceability and calibrated inspection documentation.

With an annual output exceeding 200 metric tons of finished precision components, a 98% on-time delivery record, and an 85% repeat order rate, we are the preferred machining sub-contractor for leading valve and pump manufacturers.
