What We Deliver
Valve & Pump Machining —
Prototype to Production

We specialise in the precision machining of large-diameter industrial components — particularly valve bodies, pump casings, and custom-engineered fluid control parts. From drawing review and DFM through to final inspection and export-ready dispatch, we manage the complete machining value chain.

Industrial Valve Component Machining
  • Ball, Gate, Globe, Check & Butterfly valve bodies
  • Valve bonnets, discs, seats & seat rings
  • Stems, yokes, gland flanges & packing followers
  • API 6D / ASME / DIN / BS class valve components
  • Sizes from 2" to 60" bore diameter
  • Materials: Cast Iron, Carbon Steel, SS 304/316, Duplex
Pump & Rotating Equipment Components
  • Centrifugal pump casings — split & single-piece
  • Impellers, wear rings & stage diffusers
  • Bearing housings, cartridge seals & shaft sleeves
  • Base plates, coupling guards & adaptor flanges
  • Vertical turbine pump bowl assemblies
  • Suction & discharge nozzle machining
Custom Engineering & Prototype Machining
  • Complex 3D geometries from customer drawings (2D/3D)
  • One-off prototypes to high-volume production batches
  • Design for Manufacturability (DFM) consultation
  • Reverse engineering from physical samples
  • Tooling & fixture design for special components
  • Pre-production feasibility studies — tolerance review
Material Sourcing & Turnkey Sub-Contract
  • Approved casting procurement from partner foundries
  • Forging & billet sourcing with material certificates
  • Full heat number traceability maintained through machining
  • Third-party NDT: DP, MPI, UT via accredited partners
  • Export packing & commercial document preparation
  • Consolidated sub-contract logistics coordination
