Who We Are
A Four-Decade Legacy
of Precision Engineering

Established in 1985 by an engineer-entrepreneur with a clear vision — Solanke Enterprises has grown from a single-machine workshop into one of North Karnataka's most capable large-diameter precision machining facilities. Today, we are a ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing partner serving valve OEMs, pump manufacturers, and flow equipment companies across India.

Our 10,000 sq. ft. purpose-built facility in the Gamanagatti Industrial Estate, Hubballi, operates with a team of 15+ skilled machinists, quality engineers, and tooling experts. We process raw castings, forgings, and billets into dimensionally verified, ready-to-assemble components — with full material traceability and calibrated inspection documentation.

With an annual output exceeding 200 metric tons of finished precision components, a 98% on-time delivery record, and an 85% repeat order rate, we are the preferred machining sub-contractor for leading valve and pump manufacturers.

Vision Statement
To be the most trusted large-diameter precision machining partner for valve and pump manufacturers in India — delivering components that ensure zero leakage, long service life, and absolute dimensional integrity in the most critical flow control applications.
Mission Statement
To deliver repeatable precision through ISO-governed processes, engage our clients' engineers from prototype through full production, and maintain uncompromised quality and on-time delivery as non-negotiable standards — every order, every component.
Quality Policy
"Zero defects, on-time delivery, and continuous improvement — driven by data and verified by calibrated measurement."

Our Journey
Company Milestones
1985
Founded in Hubballi
Established as a precision machining workshop, initially serving local engineering OEMs with centre lathe operations.
1995
Large-Diameter Specialisation
Invested in first horizontal boring machines, enabling valve body machining up to 600mm — entered the valve OEM supply chain.
2005
Facility Expansion
Shifted to 10,000 sq. ft. Gamanagatti Industrial Estate facility. Installed 5T EOT crane for heavy component handling.
2012
CNC VTL Investment
Commissioned ACE VTL-30plus CNC Vertical Turning Lathe — expanded capacity to 1600mm swing, serving large gate valve and API-class components.
2018
ISO 9001:2015 Certification
Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Implemented SPC, NABL-traceable calibration, and digital inspection reporting systems.
2024–25
MNC Vendor Programme
Formalized MNC vendor registration capability — export documentation, DFM engineering support, and compliance documentation fully structured.
